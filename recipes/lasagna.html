Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a delcious delicacy from the Italian region of Parma. This receipe laa dee da dee doo is best served warm and amongst friends.

This is an easy recipe that only requires 7 ingredients and an hour and a half. Buon Appetito!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ricota Cheese
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Italian Sausage

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles following the instructions on the box. Don't forget to salt the water!
  2. While you are cooking the noodles, preheat the oven to 350.
  3. Build your lasagna! Alternate layers of Noodle, Tomatoe Sauce, Mozzarella Cheese, Noodle, Tomatoe Sauce, and Ricota Cheese.
  4. Bake your lasagna at 400 degrees for 30 minutes
  5. Let your lasagna cool for 10 minutes and dig in!
