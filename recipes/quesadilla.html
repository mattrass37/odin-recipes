Quesadilla

Description

This is such an easy recipe it's crazy! This will definitely be a crowd pleaser. So easy! Just cheese and tortillas!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Two Flour Tortillas
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Heat up oil on a frying pan.
  2. Construct the quesadilla by putting the cheese in between two tortillas.
  3. Put the quesadilla into the pan and cook until the tortilla is golden brown.
  4. Flip the quesadilla to brown the other side.
