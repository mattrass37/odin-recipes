BLT

Description

The classic american sandwich for anytime. This delicious, quick recipe will definetely be a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoe
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. First toast the bread.
  2. Put mayo on one of the toasted bread slices.
  3. Finish the sandwich by adding the bacon, lettuce and tomatoe in the sandwich.
